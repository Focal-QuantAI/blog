Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our CISO-as-a-Service offerings

While often used interchangeably, there are subtle differences. CISO as a Service (CISOaaS) typically refers to a more comprehensive, outsourced security leadership solution that may include technology and managed services. Virtual CISO (vCISO) usually describes a fractional or part-time security executive who provides strategic guidance. At Field CISO Advisory, we blend the best of both models to meet your specific needs.

We can typically begin engagements within 2-3 weeks of contract signing. Our rapid onboarding process includes a comprehensive discovery phase to understand your business, current security posture, and immediate priorities. For urgent situations, we can expedite this timeline to provide immediate interim leadership.

Our services are ideal for mid-market companies (50-2,000 employees) that need strategic security leadership but aren't ready for or don't require a full-time CISO. We also serve larger enterprises needing interim coverage or specialized expertise, and smaller companies preparing for growth or compliance requirements.

We establish clear KPIs at the start of each engagement, which typically include: improved security maturity scores, reduced time to detect/respond to incidents, compliance audit results, employee security awareness metrics, and risk reduction metrics. We provide quarterly reports showing progress against these benchmarks.
