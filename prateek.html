Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our vCISO services.

While the terms are often used interchangeably, vCISO typically refers to a virtual Chief Information Security Officer who provides strategic leadership remotely, while CISOaaS (CISO as a Service) is a broader term that may include additional managed security services. Our offering combines the best of both models.

We offer flexible engagement models including monthly retainers, project-based pricing, and hourly consulting. Costs depend on the scope of services, your organization's size, and required time commitment. Most clients find our services cost 30-50% less than hiring a full-time CISO with comparable experience.

Our team has experience across multiple sectors including financial services, healthcare, technology, manufacturing, and professional services. This cross-industry perspective allows us to bring best practices from various domains to your organization.

We can typically begin engagements within 2-4 weeks of contract signing. Our streamlined onboarding process includes understanding your business objectives, current security posture, and key stakeholders to ensure rapid value delivery.

We support smooth transitions to in-house security leadership when the time is right. Our team can help define the role, assist with candidate evaluation, and ensure knowledge transfer. Many clients continue with our advisory services even after hiring a full-time CISO.
